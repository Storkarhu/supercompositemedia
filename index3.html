SUPERCOMPOSITE MEDIA

Steph Maj Swanson f.k.a. Supercomposite is principal of Supercomposite Media, a multimedia art and writing practice based in Uppsala, Sweden, specializing in critical AI discourse, creative content, public speaking, screenwriting, filmmaking, digital music composition, and film programming.

Recent Work & Current Projects

Supercomposite Media completed the "Algorithmic Grotesque" program for Uppsala Short Film Festival and continues developing multimedia projects exploring technology's impact on art and society.

Loab™

Supercomposite Media gained international recognition for the story of Loab™, an AI-generated cryptid that Forbes praised as "genuinely interesting and unsettling" and The Atlantic dubbed "a form of expression that has never existed before." The project sparked global conversations about AI ethics and creative expression, featured across media outlets including Rolling Stone, Der Spiegel, Artnet News, BBC Radio, and ABC.

To learn more about Loab™, visit loab.ai or read the original viral Twitter thread.

Suicide III

Original short film written and directed by Steph for ARRG!'s video art installation at DEF CON 31 Las Vegas (2023). The work employs deepfakes and synthetic imagery to construct a satirical counter-narrative to AI hype culture. The film has screened at The Museum of the Moving Image New York (Philip K Dick Film Festival, April 2024), Uppsala Short Film Festival (October 2024), Videomedeja festival Novi Sad Serbia (October 2024), and in a basement art gallery in Almaty, Kazakhstan.
